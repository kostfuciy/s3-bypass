# Privacy Policy for Darkbit Bypass

**Effective Date: June 13, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to the **Darkbit Bypass** application (herein referred to as "App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage (No-Logs Policy)
Darkbit Bypass is designed to protect your privacy and security. We strictly adhere to a **No-Logs Policy**. 
* **We do NOT collect, log, store, or share** your browsing history, traffic data, DNS queries, or IP addresses.
* **We do NOT require** any personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) to use the core features of the App.

## 2. VpnService Usage
The App utilizes the Android `VpnService` API to create a secure, encrypted, device-level tunnel to a remote server of your choosing. 
* This is the core functionality of the App, necessary to route your network traffic securely.
* The traffic is encrypted locally on your device and sent to your specified proxy server. 
* We do not intercept, manipulate, or monitor the contents of the traffic passing through the VPN tunnel.

## 3. Configuration Files
The App allows you to import custom configuration files (e.g., JSON formats). These files are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

If you choose to interact with these external services, your interactions are governed by Telegram's Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for the data collection practices of Telegram.

We take reasonable measures to protect the integrity of the App. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for ensuring the security of the configuration files you import into the App.
